EXCLUSIONS
Although Northwind Health Plus provides comprehensive coverage for medical, vision, and 
dental services, there are certain services and treatments that are excluded from the plan. It 
is important to understand these exclusions so that you can plan your care accordingly. 
Services Not Covered: 
Northwind Health Plus does not cover services that are not medically necessary, such as 
cosmetic surgery or elective procedures. Additionally, services or treatments that are 
experimental or investigational are not covered under this plan. 
Prescriptions Not Covered: The plan does not cover prescriptions that are not medically 
necessary, certain over-the-counter medications, or prescription medications that are used 
to enhance performance in athletics.
